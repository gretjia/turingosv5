# TuringOS Generative Economy Whitepaper Update

REAL-9 synthesis updates the launch narrative after REAL-5S through REAL-8.

Core claims:

```text
v4 does not copy v3.
v4 rebuilds v3's economic pressure under constitution.
price = signal, not truth.
market = role-specific institution, not prompt decoration.
```

Final framing:

```text
v3 taught us pressure.
v4 gives us law.
Next phase must build lawful pressure.
```

REAL-5 proves role scaffolding, not market emergence. REAL-6 moves uncertainty
earlier with TaskOutcomeMarket, keeps AttemptPrediction scripted until explicit
Class-4 ratification, and treats scheduler recommendations as observe-only.
REAL-7 demonstrates structural pressure without claiming v3-identical volume.
REAL-8 is a descriptive A/B benchmark; negative results remain valid evidence.

The launch thesis is therefore narrow: TuringOS is beginning to rebuild
generative market pressure as lawful, role-scoped, tape-visible feedback. It is
not claiming spontaneous trading, model ranking, price-as-truth, or unconstrained
DeFi behavior.
