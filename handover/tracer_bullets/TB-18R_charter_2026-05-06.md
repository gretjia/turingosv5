## §0 Why TB-18R exists (verbatim from VETO archive §A.1)

```text
TuringOS 当前 M1 benchmark 不是完整图灵机式 tape 运行，
而是"最终结果快照 + 私有 evaluator 过程"。
这违反了你最初的设计：Agent 的有意义活动必须发生在 tape 上，
能够被 read/write/predicate/ledger 审计，而不是只在 evaluator
内存和 stdout 中消失。
```

Concretely (from VETO archive §A.2):

```text
P49:
  evaluator 发生 32 次 LLM-Lean cycle；
  tool_dist = step:31 + omega_wtool:1；
  failed_branch_count = 31；
  但 ChainTape L4 只有 1 个 WorkTx；
  L4.E 真实 LLM rejection = 0。
```

Code-grounded confirmation (from VETO archive §C.1):
  - `omega_wtool` success path (lines 2317/2861) → `submit_typed_tx(WorkTx)` (lines 2457/2999) + `submit_typed_tx(VerifyTx)` (lines 2500/3046). ✅ externalized.
  - `step_partial_ok` (line 3236) → `bus.append_oracle_accepted` only (kernel.tape shadow, NOT L4). ❌ TB-7 §4 known debt.
  - `step_reject` (line 3263) → `bus.record_rejection` + `tb11_lean_error_count++`. ❌ NO L4.E.
  - `parse_fail` (line 3275) → `bus.record_rejection` + `tb11_protocol_parse_failure_count++`. ❌ NO L4.E.
  - `llm_err` (line 3289) → `tool_dist["llm_err"]++` only. ❌ NO L4.E.

Therefore the **structural defect is failure-path asymmetry**, not uniform truncation. TB-18R closes this asymmetry.
