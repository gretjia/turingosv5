# REAL-5 Role-Based Generative Scaffolding Charter

Status: ACTIVE
Risk: Class 4 package
FC: FC1 / FC2 / FC3

## Charter

REAL-5 不是 DeFi 扩展。
REAL-5 是生成脚手架。
目标是让不同角色产生可审计生成行为。

REAL-5 upgrades the G-Phase from proof-solver market probing to role-based generative market scaffolding. The intended evidence path is role-scoped view -> typed role output -> predicate/audit gate -> ChainTape/CAS fossil -> feedback/autopsy.

## FC Mapping

- FC1: every externalized role action routes to L4 / L4.E / CAS.
- FC2: role assignment and PromptCapsule role/view identity replay from genesis or batch manifest plus ChainTape/CAS.
- FC3: role performance, no-trade/no-verify/no-challenge reasons, and autopsy evidence are derived materialized views, not hidden sources.

## Forbidden List

- no price-as-truth
- no forced trade
- no private CoT recording
- no raw-log broadcast
- no ghost liquidity
- no automatic tool/predicate mutation
- no real funds

## Ship Interpretation

REAL-5 proves role-based scaffolding and tape-visible decisions. It does not claim model ranking, forced trading, or emergent role differentiation.
