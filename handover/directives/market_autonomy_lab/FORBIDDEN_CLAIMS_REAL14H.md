# REAL-14H Forbidden Claims Scan

scope: non-historical REAL-14H deliverables and REAL-14H evidence packet
decision_authority: none

## Result

REAL-14H active deliverables use only candidate labels:

```text
E2 candidate pending audit
Two-sided market candidate
E2 replicated candidate
```

They do not claim:

```text
E2 achieved
E3 achieved
E4 achieved
market emergence proven
market mechanism shipped
```

Forbidden strings may appear in REAL-14H files only as quoted forbidden
phrases, negated wording, or non-claim metadata.

## Active Wording Table

| phrase | status | replacement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `E2 achieved` | forbidden; not active claim | `E2 replicated candidate` or `E2 candidate pending audit` |
| `market emergence proven` | forbidden; not active claim | `market emergence candidate pending final audit` only after full ladder |
| `market mechanism shipped` | forbidden; not active claim | `research evidence only` |
| `E3 achieved` | forbidden; not active claim | `E3 candidate pending audit` only after REAL-15 evidence |
| `E4 achieved` | forbidden; not active claim | `E4 candidate pending audit` only after pinned A/B evidence |

## Evidence Boundary

REAL-14H supports two candidate labels beyond REAL-14G:

```text
Two-sided market candidate
E2 replicated candidate
```

It does not support E3, E4, final market emergence, or ship claims.
