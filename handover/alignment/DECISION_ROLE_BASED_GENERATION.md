# DECISION: Role-Based Generation

REAL-5 adopts role-based generation as the next G-Phase scaffold.

Decision:

- Solver generates proof.
- Verifier generates verification judgment.
- Challenger generates counterexample / challenge.
- Trader generates market decision.
- MarketMaker generates liquidity decision.
- ArchitectAI generates tool/predicate proposal.
- VetoAI generates veto decision.

The objective is not forced trade. The objective is typed, role-scoped, tape-visible generation.
